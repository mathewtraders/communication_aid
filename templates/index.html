Communication Aid

COMMUNICATION—HUMAN-CONNECTION. The disabled and partially paralyzed people face lot of trouble to communicate with people . Partially paralyzed can move there whole hand and figures, but cannot speak properly . So, they need a proper medium for communicating with people around them and make themselves feel comfortable. “I CAN’T CHANGE THE DIRECTION OF THE WIND,BUT I CAN ADJUST MY SAILS TO ALWAYS REACH MY DESTINATION.”

We have a Innotive idea for the problem mentioned above . HERE BASIC IMPORTANCE IS TO PROVIDE THEM A COMMUNICATION MEDIUM. So, a person can communicate by using his finger for tapping a touchpad ,by which they can produce a morsecode sequence. This sequence of will be converted into ENGLISH, So that we will get the final output as SPEECH ,which others can understand and support them for their needs.

Guide

  • Find the white area for tapping in execution section.
  • Use your mouse pad to generate the morse sequence via tapping.
  • Tap or click on the whitespace only, to generate MORSE SEQUENCE
    • Instructions:
    • single tap or click for dot(.)
    • Double tap or click for dash(-)
    • 2 finger tap or right mouse click for space between letters.
    • Give two spaces between words and single space between letters in a word.

      • Sample:
      Input=> .....|.||..|...||.-||-...|---|-.-- ( | => single space; || => double space)
      Output=> HE IS A BOY
